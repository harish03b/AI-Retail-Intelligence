# Dataset Acquisition Strategy

## Objective

The Enterprise Retail Decision Intelligence Platform uses multiple datasets collected from different public sources.

Each dataset has been selected based on a specific business requirement instead of simply maximizing the amount of available data.

---

# Dataset Selection Principles

The datasets should:

- Represent real-world retail operations.
- Support machine learning models.
- Enable recommendation systems.
- Improve forecasting accuracy.
- Support customer analytics.
- Integrate with Generative AI.
- Provide structured and unstructured data.

---

# Selected Datasets

## Walmart Sales Forecasting

Purpose

- Demand Forecasting
- Revenue Prediction
- Weekly Sales Analysis

Business Questions

- Which products will sell next week?
- Which stores require more inventory?
- How do holidays affect sales?

---

## Instacart Market Basket Analysis

Purpose

- Recommendation System

Business Questions

- Which products are purchased together?
- Which products should be recommended?

---

## Online Retail II

Purpose

- Customer Segmentation

Business Questions

- Who are our premium customers?
- Which customers are likely to churn?

---

## Holiday Dataset

Purpose

Improve forecasting accuracy.

Business Questions

- How do festivals impact demand?
- How should inventory change during holidays?

---

## Weather Dataset

Purpose

External business features.

Business Questions

- Does rainfall reduce store sales?
- How does temperature influence demand?

---

## Company Documents

Purpose

Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)

Examples

- Product Manuals
- Inventory SOP
- Return Policy
- Employee Handbook
- Supplier Agreements

Business Questions

- What is our return policy?
- Explain inventory guidelines.
- Summarize supplier contracts.

---

# Dataset Integration Strategy

The datasets will be integrated into a unified enterprise retail database.

The integration process includes:

- Schema Mapping
- Data Cleaning
- Feature Engineering
- Data Validation
- Metadata Generation

---

# Expected Outcome

The combined datasets will provide a realistic enterprise environment for:

- Machine Learning
- Business Intelligence
- RAG
- AI Agents
- Dashboard Analytics
